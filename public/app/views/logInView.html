Why did I build this?

To tell the truth, I built this because of porn. My friend Chris and I are Christians and we don't think we should be looking at porn (for a very large number of reasons). Chris set up a spreadsheet on Google Docs when 2013 started and we started tracking each time we looked at porn. When we don't look at porn for a certain amount of time, we go out and get pie.

This is basically the same thing as that, but with a shinier front end. You could track a few different metrics to try to find certain trends - different times of the week that the metrics fail, if certain metrics are correlated to other metrics (do I tend to look at porn after I have a fight with my wife? do I tend to look at porn on the weekends? does this change if I spend more time reading the Bible?)

In other words... this could be considered an app that tries to get you to answer the question "am I becoming someone that I want to be?"
